OUR UNIVERSITY

Established in the year 1994, Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya is a co-education and English medium school located in Bijinapally Mandal, Telangana. The classes are up to 12th standard and the admission is done through an entrance test, JNVST to class 6th. The school is managed by Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti and the Government of India. We aim to impart quality education to students who are mainly from a rural background and 30% of seats are reserved for girls students.
